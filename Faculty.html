WELCOME TO OUR FACULTY

BASIC & APPLIEDl SCIENCES

The College of Applied Science offers a dynamic learning environment, integrating theoretical knowledge with practical applications across various disciplines. Its specialized curriculum equips students with hands-on experience and industry-relevant skills, fostering a seamless transition into the workforce upon graduation. Through state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty, the College of Applied Science prioritizes innovation and real-world problem-solving to prepare students for success in their chosen fields.

BSC. EDUCATION

The College of Education is dedicated to preparing future educators and administrators through comprehensive programs that emphasize pedagogy, curriculum development, and educational leadership. With a focus on fostering a supportive learning environment and cultivating innovative teaching methodologies, the College of Education strives to empower students to positively impact the realm of education.

BSC. HEALTH SCIENCE

The College of Health Science offers an interdisciplinary approach to studying healthcare, encompassing fields like nursing, public health, and allied health professions. By providing rigorous academic training combined with practical clinical experiences, the college prepares students to address complex health challenges and make meaningful contributions to the well-being of individuals and communities.

BSC.HUMANITIES

The College of Humanities provides a rich academic environment that delves into the diverse aspects of human culture, history, language, and thought. Through critical thinking, creative expression, and interdisciplinary studies, students in the College of Humanities gain a deep understanding of society and the human experience, fostering a well-rounded education for global citizenship.

BSC. SCHOOL OF GRADUATE STUDIES

The School of Graduate Studies serves as an academic hub dedicated to advanced research, innovation, and scholarly pursuits across various disciplines. With specialized programs, mentorship, and a focus on cutting-edge research, the School of Graduate Studies prepares students for leadership roles in academia, industry, and beyond.
